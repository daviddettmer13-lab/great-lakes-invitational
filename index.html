⛳ Tournament Overview

The Great Lakes Invitational is a 4-round team tournament pitting Team Michigan against Team Superior across 4 Michigan golf courses, August 27–29, 2026. Teams consist of 10 players each, determined by draft on August 26.

🏆 Point System

  • Match Win — Team with more holes won in a matchup earns 1 team point
  • Aggregate Holes — Team with most total holes won (or highest total Stableford in Round 2) across all matchups earns 1 team point
  • Closest to the Pin — Team of the winning player on each par 3 CTP earns 0.5 team points (cash prize for the individual)
  • Skins — Team whose player/group posts the outright lowest raw score on any hole earns 0.5 team points. Ties = no skin awarded.
  • Largest Margin (Round 4 only) — Team with the biggest individual hole differential earns 1 bonus team point

📊 Round Formats

Round 1 — The Crown Golf Club: 2v2 Scramble. All players hit from the tee; the team picks the best shot and plays from that spot, repeating until holed. 5 matchups of 2v2 cover all 10 players per team. Scoring: Match Win + Aggregate Holes + CTP + Skins.

Round 2 — Sugar Loaf Old Course: Stableford Combined. Individual stroke play with Stableford points per hole. Each 2-player matchup adds both players' Stableford scores per hole — the team with the higher combined total wins that hole. Team with more holes won earns 1 point. Team with the highest total Stableford across all matchups earns 1 Aggregate point. CTP and Skins are determined by lowest raw stroke count.

Triple+: 0pts · Double: 1pt · Bogey: 2pts · Par: 3pts · Birdie: 5pts · Eagle: 7pts

Round 3 — Manitou Passage: Alt-Shot / Scramble Combo. All players hit tee shots → scramble to best drive → alternate shots on approach → scramble putting. Scored identically to Round 1: Match Win + Aggregate Holes + CTP + Skins.

Round 4 — Grand Traverse Resort: 1v1 Singles Match Play with blind ranking. Players ranked by individual points earned across Rounds 1–3; matchups assigned without knowing your opponent. Win your individual match = 1 team point. Biggest hole differential across all matches = bonus 1 team point. CTP and Skins still in play.

🎯 Tiebreaker

If teams are tied at the end of Round 4, the winner is determined by Total Team Holes Won across all rounds (CTP and Skins points excluded from this calculation).

🥇 MVP Award

The player who earned the most individual points for their team across all 4 rounds wins the MVP Award. Points are tracked at the individual level throughout the tournament. The winner receives a cash prize awarded at the trophy ceremony.

📋 General Rules

  • Preferred Lies — You may fluff your lie anywhere on the course except in a hazard. A bunker is a hazard.
  • Out of Bounds / Lost Ball — Drop at the point of entry into the hazard, 2 club lengths no closer to the hole. 1 stroke penalty (lying 2, hitting 3).
  • Conceded Holes — A hole may be conceded, but all 18 holes must be played.
  • Maximum Score — Double par on any hole (e.g. max 6 on a par 3, 8 on a par 4, 10 on a par 5).
  • Gimmes — Within the leather; must be conceded by opponent.
  • Commissioner's Decision — The Commissioner makes the final ruling on any rule dispute. All rebuttals must be submitted through team captains and will be considered for board review.
